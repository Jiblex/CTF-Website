Hamlet.

Hamlet, the Prince of Denmark, newphew to the usurping Claudius and son of King Hamlet. 
Juliet, a lover from a different dimension, sent to save Hamlet from his inevitable fate.

Act I: Juliet's Plan and Hamlet's Heartbreak.

Scene I: The Conflict.

[Enter Hamlet and Juliet]

Juliet: You lying stupid fatherless big smelly half-witted coward! You are as
stupid as the difference between a handsome rich brave hero and thyself!

Hamlet: Thou art the sum of a cat and a bad dirty cowardly worried pig.

[A pause]

Juliet: Speak your mind!


[Exeunt]
